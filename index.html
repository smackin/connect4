⋰ Connect - Four ⋱

Rules of the Game

Player 1 and Player 2 alternate.
On each turn, click the top column where you would like a piece dropped down a column.
Once a player gets four-in-a-row (horiz, vert, or diag) they're the Winner!
Unless you fill the board, then we have a tie and you must play again.
Good Luck!
